What it extracts

Issuer, invoice number, invoice date, total amount, VAT, currency, and line items.

Who it’s for

Accounting workflows, AP automation, procurement pipelines, and custom internal tools.

Why it wins

No UI bloat. No enterprise sales call. Just a stable endpoint for digital invoice PDFs.

One request

curl -X POST \
  -H 'Content-Type: application/pdf' \
  -H 'X-API-Key: free-demo-key' \
  --data-binary @invoice.pdf \
  https://your-api.example.com/extract
